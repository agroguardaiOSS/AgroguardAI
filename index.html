Our Focus

The Intelligence Layer

Standard AI models fail in the specific, localized contexts of rural farming. We are engineering the guardrails.

Multilingual Alignment

Evaluating and fine-tuning models to safely process and generate agricultural advisory in Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, and Fulfulde without critical data loss.

Hallucination Mitigation

In agriculture, a wrong AI output can ruin a harvest. We are developing strict validation architectures to ensure deterministic, fact-grounded responses.
